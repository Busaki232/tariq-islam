Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Global Muslims Connect mobile application (“the App”), developed and published by Busaki Investments (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

Global Muslims Connect is a community-focused mobile application that provides features such as messaging, community discussions, events, and audio/video calling.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email address, name or username, profile photo).
  • Content you provide, including messages and community posts.
  • Metadata required to operate core features (timestamps, conversation IDs).
  • Device and app information for reliability and security (diagnostics, push notification tokens).
  • Location information only if you choose to use location-based features (such as nearby mosques).

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain core app functionality.
  • To enable messaging, calls, and community features.
  • To improve stability, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To send notifications when you have enabled them.

Sharing of Information

We may share data with trusted service providers used to operate the App, such as hosting, database, notifications, and call infrastructure providers (for example, Supabase, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and Daily.co). We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data as described below.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by visiting: Delete Account.

Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Global Muslims Connect application published on Google Play by Busaki Investments and does not apply to any other apps, websites, or services.

Children’s Privacy

Global Muslims Connect is intended for users aged 18 and above. The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at support@global-muslims-connect.com.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@global-muslims-connect.com.
